# Laboratory / Diagnostics System (LIS)

## Objective
Establish a professional Request-to-Result-to-Revenue workflow that ensures clinical accuracy and financial integrity.

## Subscription Tier
- **Standard** (Tier 2) or higher.

## Scope
### 1. Test Management & Command Center
- Status-driven Dashboard: Order Queue, Processing, To Publish, and Archive.
- Priority Management: Urgent requests (STAT) with visual pinning.
- Digital Result Entry: Structured parameter capture with normal range validation.

### 2. AI Lab Assistant
- **External Report Parsing**: AI-driven OCR to import lab reports from third-party facilities.
- **Diagnostic Insights**: Automated abnormal finding detection and clinical interpretation.
- **Trend Analysis**: Comparative analysis of historical results for predictive health scoring.

### 3. Revenue Integration
- **Lab Billing Center**: Centralized queue for financial reconciliation of all completed tests.
- **Mandatory Billing**: System prevents report publication until payment status is confirmed (HIPAA & Operational compliance).
- **Automated Invoicing**: Single-click conversion from clinical result to financial invoice.

## Workflow Integration
- **Input**: Digital Doctor Orders or AI-imported external reports.
- **Control**: Technologist authorization and validation.
- **Output**: Billed Invoices, Verified Digital Reports, and Patient Portal synchronization.

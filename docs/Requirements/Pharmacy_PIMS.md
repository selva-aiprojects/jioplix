# Pharmacy Information Management (PIMS)

## Objective
Automate pharmacy dispensing and inventory while ensuring 100% revenue reconciliation via prescription-linked billing.

## Subscription Tier
- **Standard** (Tier 2) or higher.

## Scope
### 1. Inventory & Procurement
- **Stock Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of quantity on hand.
- **Batch & Expiry Control**: Proactive alerts for near-expiry medication.
- **Automated Reordering**: Stock level warnings with one-click purchase order generation.

### 2. Dispensing & Fulfillment
- **Prescription Queue**: Real-time dashboard for pharmacists to fulfill doctor prescriptions.
- **Fulfillment Validation**: Digital confirmation of drug, dosage, and quantity before dispensing.

### 3. Pharmacy Revenue Center (New)
- **POS Billing Integration**: Automated invoice generation from clinical prescriptions.
- **Mandatory Payment Bridge**: Drugs are only marked as "Dispensed" in the system once payment is reconciled or insurance is authorized.
- **Revenue Dashboard**: Tracking of daily pharmacy collection and pending settlements.

### 4. Clinical Safety
- **Drug Interaction Alerts**: Basic safety checks during fulfillment.
- **Allergy Validation**: Sync with patient clinical records to prevent adverse reactions.

## Integration Flow
- **Input**: Digital Prescription from OPD/IPD modules.
- **Process**: Pharmacist validation and inventory decrement.
- **Output**: Reconciled Invoice and Patient Dispense Record.
